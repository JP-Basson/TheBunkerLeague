League Info

Fan run league which will run for 6 weeks from the 27th of August to the 28th of November. With weekly games consisting of different point levels and vastly different missions we aim to offer a fun league that is open to all players.

schedule

Location and Time

This league is run from Battle Bunker Cavendish, default games are on Wednesdays from about 7pm. However in the week of your games you are free to schedule them where and when you wish. Just as long as the games are reported within the weekly times.

About Bunker League

We are a fan run league, working in affiliation with Battle Bunker. We aim to have this league every couple of months to try and promote the Warhammer scene in Cape Town! For enquiries please contact us at battlebunkerleague@gmail.com
